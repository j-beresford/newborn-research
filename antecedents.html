Antcedents

What can we do before a behaviour takes place to make it more likely to occur. These are less familiar than consequences but equally important. 1. Playfull challenges: I bet you can't put your shoes on in under 2 mins, you'd have to be a super hero to do that. 2. Offer assistance 3. Say please 4. Gentle tone of voice 5. Be clear and specific: pick up five items of clothing 6. Offer choices: do you want your red coat or blue jumper?

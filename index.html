Welcome

This website is primarily designed to help your baby become a better sleeper. It is based on Dr Weissbluth's 'Healthy Sleep Habits, Happy Child' and sets out the steps to take right from birth. The aim is for your baby to develop the essential self-soothing skills that will allow both parents and babies to get a good night's sleep.

The parenting section outlines behaviour change tehniques for older children. It is based on a coursera course, Everyday parenting, by Alan E. Kazdin of Yale.
